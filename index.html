Welcome to Periodic Table Battleship! You will be facing off against a computer in a classic game of Battleship with an educational twist! Follow the instructions below to discover what it is!

  1. Enter names for yourself and the AI. Defaults are provided.
  2. Two Periodic Tables will appear. The one on top is your "grid" and the one below it is the computer's.
  3. Place 17 ships on your Periodic Table. Element squares will turn black as ships are placed on them.
  4. Click on an element square to begin battling.
  5. During each round, select an element square on the computer's Periodic Table to launch a missile at it. The computer will then counter-attack.
  6. Element sqaures will turn green to indicate a 'Hit' and red to indicate a 'Miss'. Whomever sinks all of their opponent's ships first wins!
